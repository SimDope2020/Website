Pegasus

Pagesus Exotic Car Dealership was founded in 2017, 16 June. The owner and mastermind behind the brand "Mave Rick", is a retired Web Developer, who has since then dedicated all of his time to an amazing car collection. Apart from being lucky, Mave has not based his success and fortune on winning the lotto, "My fortune is based on the love a have for my collection.".

Mave started his private collection at the age of 23, when he's dad gave him ownership of his own new wheels. Mave found his calling as a collection was accumulating years on, and finding himself among the elite car clubs to show off this prestige collection.

As the years gone by, Mave has gotten ridiculous amount of offers for the cars and members of the various car clubs asking him on advice to keep, or sell. Mave took the initiative and laid down trhe foundation, by opening up a platform to which owners of high end exotic cars could show off and advertise, using Mave's reputation. Now Pegasus Exotic Car Dealership has a local and postive footprint on the market. It boasts amzing collections ranging from Pagani, to Bugatti, etc. Finance can be arranged on vehicles, including warranty plans. full valet services available as well as body work (restoration).
